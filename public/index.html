About OKQR

(pronounced as ‘Okay QR’)

OKQR is a Simple, Innovative and Affordable QR Marketing Platform for Small and Medium Enterprises.

Using OKQR Mobile platform, generate a customized QR Code for your business within minutes and associate details like:

  •   Your Catalog of Products & Services
  •   Special Offers & Events
  •   Contact Details with Location
  •   Videos, Social Media & Other links
    • ...  and much more!

Print your QR on Business Cards, Posters, Pamphlets, Signages, Hoardings and any other marketing material to reach out to your customers.

Allow your customers to Scan & Share your Business information with their mobiles, using a simple QR Code.
